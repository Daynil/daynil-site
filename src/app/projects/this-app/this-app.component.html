Personal Site

What is it?

I created this website as a single page application as a way to showcase what I believe is the best of the web - snappy response, slick and interactive interface, and a clean, simple, responsive design. Although traditional backend-heavy websites still have their place, I believe a well thought out single page application architecture is the way forward in the presentation of most websites. With this architecture, you get increased interactivity and immediate response times, as well as more flexibility in design and organization of code. I believe the slightly increased up-front load times, which can be minimized with intelligent use of preloading, are more than offset by all of these benefits.

Tech Stack

  • Front end: Angular 4+
  • Back end: Node and Express
  • Hosting: Digital Ocean
